Welcome to the official site of the Library of Things Sale! Each month, on the 2nd Saturday and Sunday, we at Friends of the Palo Alto Library open our doors to host a used book sale. On this site you can learn the purpose of the sale, its location, what's being sold and its pricing.

Our Purpose

We've amassed over 50,000 items from donations from the community, estates, companies, and our very own Palo Alto libraries. With these donations and your purchases, we are able to hold this book sale and put 100% of its proceeds back into our local libraries!

Book Sale

When: Every 2nd Saturday & Sunday of each month
Start time: 8am
Where: Friends of the Palo Alto Library--4000 Middlefield Rd. Palo Alto, CA 94303

Know Before You Go

To enter the book sale:

Proceed to the Main Room
Grab a numbered ticket--2/person
The number on the ticket corresponds with your place in line
Tickets will only be distributed between 8-11am
After 11am, you will need to join the latter part of the line
Once inside, customers are allowed to purchase 12 books per entry

To buy more books, return to the end of the line or grab a numbered ticket (if possible)
Please note: if you miss the time when your ticket number is allowed to enter the Main Room you will forfeit your place in line.

Notes

Rooms K6 & K7 (bargain books) open one hour before the Main Room on Saturdays.
On Sundays, prices in the bargain room are half-off
Gift certificates available in $5 denominations.
Donations of large books should be brought to the Main Room

Come out and enrich your lives while also supporting Palo Alto's local libraries! We thank you for your participation.
